How to Delete Your Account

Habesh Date allows users to permanently delete their accounts directly inside the application.

Deletion Steps

  • Open the Habesh Date app
  • Go to Settings
  • Select Account Sessions
  • Select Reason for deletion
  • Tap Delete Account
  • Confirm account deletion

What Happens After Deletion

Your profile, uploaded content, and associated personal data will be removed from active systems.

Subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel subscriptions billed through Google Play or PayPal.
